Why list your venue

Found by the right people, at the right moment.

On the walkers’ map

Your venue shows up to walkers planning and following routes nearby, right when they are deciding where to stop.

Real visits

See real walkers who physically arrived, confirmed by location and time, on a simple dashboard. No vanity metrics.

Rewards that pull people in

Offer a free coffee or a Sunday-roast deal. Walkers spend the credits they earn, so you become a place they are steered toward.

Walker-friendly by design

You reach an outdoors-first, local audience already out and about, not people scrolling at home.

Fires when it suits you

Trigger rewards on rain, a sunny weekend or a quiet weekday lunch, to bring walkers in when you need them.

Your dashboard

Track arrivals and redemptions in near real time, and tune your reward whenever you like.

How it works

Live in about ten minutes.

1

List your venue

Tell us the basics and pick a reward, or let our reward engine design one for you. Accept it in one tap.

2

Walkers find you on their route

Your venue and reward appear on the map for walkers heading your way.

3

They redeem in person

By geofence, QR or an 8-character code (with a code fallback for poor signal). You confirm, the visit is verified.

4

You see what is working

Real visits and redemptions land on your dashboard so you can see the return.

What you get

A welcome kit, posted to your door.

Every venue gets the physical bits that make Togro visible on the high street, branded and ready to put up.

Window sticker

“Togro Approved Venue” for the door, plus a “Credits Accepted Here” decal.

QR poster & sticker

An A4/A5 QR poster and a counter sticker so walkers can redeem on the spot.

Table talker

A counter card that explains how walkers redeem their reward.

Staff guide + login

A one-page guide for confirming redemptions, and a dashboard login.
