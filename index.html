How it works

Spot it. Report it. Earn. Redeem.

Every report is a real person who physically arrived, validated by GPS, dwell time and the community. Clean updates in, real rewards out.

01

Spot

Out on a route, you see something that matters, livestock, a fallen tree, a flooded path, a broken gate.

02

Report

One tap, a photo and your live location. GPS-stamped, under 25m accuracy or it's rejected.

03

Earn

Confirmed by the route service and fellow walkers, your contribution earns credits, the more verified, the more it's worth.

04

Redeem

Spend credits at rural pubs, cafés and farm shops. GPS unlocks the reward when you arrive, no codes, no tills.

Why it's different

Every report shows
its confidence.

A hazard you can't trust is just noise. Togro shows type, distance, age, confidence and verification on every single report, so you know what's real before you set off.

  • GPS-validated. Rejected if accuracy is worse than 25m or dwell is under 60 seconds.
  • Community-confirmed. Confidence climbs as more walkers verify the same report.
  • Farmer-verified. Livestock and land updates checked against the people who work the land.

About Togro

Built for the countryside,
