How you move up

You climb two ways.

Share what you see

Flooding, livestock, blocked paths, open gates, good routes. Accurate reports that other walkers confirm move you up. You rise by being right, not by how much you post.

Bring people in

Invite friends, share your routes, get your walking group onto Togro. The more you grow the community, the higher you climb. This is how we put the countryside on the map together.

Credits are separate from your stage. Credits are rewards you spend at local pubs, cafes and farm shops, and spending them never changes your standing. Your stage is about being part of the community.

What you get

More than a badge.

Free Togro+

Offline maps, live trip sharing and priority alerts, on us, for as long as you are a member.

Founding Member status

Join before launch and the badge is yours for good. It never expires and comes with founding perks.

Credits to spend

Collect credits for what you share and spend them at countryside pubs, cafes and farm shops on the map.

Group walks and meet-ups

Local walks and get-togethers, organised with and for the community. Meet your kind of people.

First to everything

Try new features before anyone else and get a direct line to the team to help shape them.

Recognition as you climb

Standing that means something, all the way to Trailblazer, with a real say in how Togro grows.

Who it is for

If you know and love your local paths.

Keen walkers

Locals who know the routes, the conditions and the best stops along the way.

Groups and ramblers

Walking groups and rambling clubs who want member perks and a shared map.

Dog walkers

Out every day, first to spot a flooded path, a closed gate or livestock in a field.

Local champions

Anyone proud of their patch who wants more people enjoying it, safely.
