Who can join

Three ways in.

You do not need a huge following. You need to know and love your patch, and want more people enjoying it, safely.

Content creators

You make videos, posts or blogs about the outdoors and the countryside. Bring your audience along.

Photographers

You capture the trails, the light and the wild places. Show people where to go and what to look for.

Passionate about your area

No following needed. You know your region inside out and want to represent it and bring people in.

The ambassador journey

Explorer to Ambassador.

Six stages, from your first walk to the face of Togro in your region. You climb two ways: by sharing what you spot, and by spreading the word and bringing people on board. Never by spending. Teal is exploring, lime is leading.

How you move up

You climb two ways.

Spread the word

Share Togro with your audience and your area. Bring walkers, friends and your community on board. The more you grow it, the higher you climb. This is how we put the countryside on the map together.

Represent your patch

Post routes and photos, and share what is really happening on the paths you know. Be the voice of Togro in your region, the person people turn to for where to go.

Credits are separate from your stage. Credits are rewards you spend at local pubs, cafes and farm shops, and spending them never changes your standing. Your stage is about how you represent and grow Togro.

What you get

More than a badge.

Free Togro+

Offline maps, live trip sharing and priority alerts, on us, for as long as you are an ambassador.

An ambassador kit

Ready-made assets, route artwork and social tiles so your content looks the part from day one.

Your referral code

Earn credits and rewards for every walker you bring to Togro, tracked to you.

Founding Ambassador status

Join before launch and the badge is yours for good. It never expires and comes with founding perks.

Credits to spend

Collect credits and spend them at countryside pubs, cafes and farm shops on the map.

Early access and a say

First to try new features, with a direct line to the team to help shape them.
