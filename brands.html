For outdoor and rural brands

Reach walkers out on the trail.

Togro is where people are when they are walking the British countryside. Partner with us to put your brand in front of an engaged outdoor audience, fund rewards they actually redeem, and back the places and communities your customers care about. Every visit is from a real person who turned up, confirmed by GPS, so you see what really happened.

Why partner

Get in front of the right people, at the right moment.

An engaged outdoor audience

Walkers who are out in the countryside, planning routes and looking for good places to stop. Exactly the people most outdoor and rural brands want to reach.

Rewards people actually use

Fund rewards walkers earn and redeem in real life. No coupons left to expire, just real visits you can see.

Numbers you can trust

Every visit is from someone who physically arrived, confirmed by GPS. You see real footfall and redemptions, not vanity metrics.

Purpose that is real

Back the countryside your customers love. Support safer access, local places and the people who look after the land.

Your brand, done tastefully

No banner spam. Your brand shows up where it fits, on routes, rewards and regions, in a way walkers welcome.

Totals only, never names

You see how campaigns perform in aggregate. We never share who individual walkers are.
